Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Datuk Au Kok Huei was appointed as the Chief Executive Officer and Director of the Company on 16th June, 2020. He holds a Master of Business Administration degree majoring in Marketing and Investment and a Bachelor of Business Administration degree from The University of Arkansas, U.S.A. He is conferred with Darjah Pangkuan Seri Melaka (DPSM), which carries the title Datuk by the Melaka Yang di-Pertua Negeri on November 2020. Datuk Au’s career span includes being the COO and CEO of a few public listed companies involved in various industries including international food and beverage, property development, trading, and most importantly, bereavement care. As the founding member and driving force behind City Centre Columbarium, he leads the company in strategic planning for sales & marketing, training, business development and operations management. Geared with his vast experience and also as a testament of his expert knowledge in the bereavement care industry, Datuk Au was appointed as Advisor and invited to be a Key Speaker for the Asia Funeral Conference and Expo (Hong Kong) in year 2009 and 2011.


Executive Director

Mr. Tan King Aun was appointed as the Executive Director of the Company on 30th July, 2020. He holds a Master of Business Administration degree specializing in Strategic Marketing from The University of Hull (UK), and a Diploma in Management Studies from HKMA/HK Polytechnic University. He was a Member of The Chartered Institute of Marketing (UK) (MCIM), and an Associate of The Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (UK) (ACIArb). Among his notable professional and management experiences were his appointment as General Manager (Hong Kong) for Royal Selangor (HK) Ltd from 1987 to 1995, and in the Hong Leong Industries Bhd subsidiaries, as Head of Projects Sales for Guocera Marketing Sdn Bhd (1995 to 2015) and General Manager (Sales) for Hume Cemboard Industries Sdn Bhd (2015 to 2017).


Marketing & Operations

Ms. Rane Lim See Leng joined the Company on November 2020. She holds a Diploma in Mass Communication and was also a Council Member and Community Member of Gold Thread International Product Lounge (China), Malaysia Retailer-Chain Association, and ASEAN Retail Chains & Franchise Federation.


Ms. Lim has over 15 years of experience in the area of marketing, advertising and promotion, of which she was involved in the bereavement care industry for 3 years. She currently handles and leads the overall Marketing and Operations functions at City Centre Columbarium.


Business Development & Sales Admin

Mr. Martin Shek Wai Hong joined the Company on October 2020. His past working experience includes 3 years in the bereavement care industry and 8 years in property development, information technology and education at various areas of responsibilities involving in sales admin, credit control, marketing and business development. Backed by the knowledge and experience he garnered from the bereavement care industry, Mr Shek is currently responsible for City Centre Columbarium’s Sales Admin, Credit Control and Business Development.
