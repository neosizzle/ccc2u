Amenities & Facilities

For visitors’ comfort and relaxation, there are amenities in the building including a Café and a Sky Park, and sofas at designated rest areas clustering around the spacious Concourses at each floor. Our Sky Park is professionally landscaped and is filled with blossoming flowers and plants. All areas are guarded by 24-hour security, a high-tech system of premises monitoring and centralised computer access control on all floors.
