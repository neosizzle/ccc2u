CITY CENTRE COLUMBARIUM

The idea of a prestigious Columbarium in the heart of Kuala Lumpur city centre was conceptualised after due diligence in various aspects of planning, design, innovation, technology & expertise were explored and digested. CITY CENTRE COLUMBARIUM is strategically located in Sungai Besi, Kuala Lumpur, which is a short 5 minutes' drive from the renowned Petronas Twin Towers. Scheduled to commence construction in the year 2021, this CITY CENTRE COLUMBARIUM, being officially registered as a Memorial Park, will rise 15 floors and anchored by 4 levels of basement for parking and service space. This unique Columbarium will be one of the most stylish facilities in Malaysia and will be managed with high-tech driven systems. It is constructed to endure for generations, harmonising the magnificent architectural craftsmanship with the religious beliefs and cultural practices of the urbanites. The City Centre Columbarium reflects the harmonious blend of light and shadow in the realm of life and sustainability. The Columbarium will be standing proudly in Kuala Lumpur overlooking the city from a high vantage point, playing an important role of preserving the memory of loved ones.
